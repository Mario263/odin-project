Burger Recipe

Back to Recipes

This burger recipe features a juicy beef patty topped with fresh lettuce, tomato, and a special sauce.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Salt and pepper
  • Burger buns
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato, sliced
  • Onion, sliced
  • Cheese slices (optional)
  • Special sauce (mayonnaise, ketchup, mustard)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the grill or stovetop pan over medium-high heat.
  2. Form the ground beef into patties and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Cook the patties for about 4-5 minutes on each side, or until they reach your desired doneness.
  4. If using cheese, place a slice on each patty during the last minute of cooking to melt.
  5. Toast the burger buns on the grill or in a toaster.
  6. Assemble the burgers by placing the patty on the bottom bun, followed by lettuce, tomato, onion, and a dollop of special sauce.
  7. Top with the other half of the bun.
  8. Serve immediately with your favorite sides.

Enjoy your delicious homemade burger!

Feel free to customize with your favorite toppings and condiments.
