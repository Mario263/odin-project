Lasagna Recipie

Back to Recipes

This classic lasagna is layered with rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and gooey cheese.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Onion, chopped
  • Garlic, minced
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Tomato paste
  • Basil and oregano
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Eggs
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a skillet, cook the ground beef with chopped onion and minced garlic until browned.
  3. Add canned tomatoes, tomato paste, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  4. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese with eggs, salt, and pepper.
  5. Spread a layer of meat sauce in a baking dish. Layer with lasagna noodles, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella cheese.
  6. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with mozzarella and Parmesan on top.
  7. Bake for 45 minutes or until bubbly and golden brown.
  8. Let it rest for 10 minutes before serving.
  9. Enjoy your delicious homemade lasagna!
